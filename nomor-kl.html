Yang terjawab =46

Soal salah.....× -1 =......
A) 31 × 2 = 62 di kurangi 46 = 15
15 × -1 = -15
62 - (-15) = 47 (A salah)

B) 32 ×2 = 64
14 × -1 = -14
64 - (-14) =50 (B salah)

C) 33 × 2 = 66
13 × -1 = -13
66 - (-13) = 53 (C benar)

Jadi jawabnya C
