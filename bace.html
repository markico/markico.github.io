19. Nih bahasa ingris ya.. :) 1.The bace face of prism is a RHOMBUS of diagonals 7cm and 14cm.Find the volume of the prism if its height is 15cm! Ty


wide rhombus = 1/2 × d₁ × d₂
d₁ = 7 cm
d₂ = 14 cm
Wide : 1/2 × 7 × 14 = 49 cm²

height = 15 cm

Volume prism = area of the base rhombus × height
V = 49 × 15
V = 735 cm³
