Eksponen!

[tex] \bold{ \color{red} {a}^{m} \times {a}^{n} = {a}^{(m + n)} }[/tex]

[tex] \bold{ \color{blue} {a}^{m} \div {a}^{n} = {a}^{(m - n)} }[/tex]

[tex] {12}^{22} \div {12}^{11} [/tex]

[tex] {12}^{(22 - 11)} [/tex]

[tex] = {12}^{11} [/tex]

[tex] {23}^{4} \times {23}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] {23}^{(4 + 2)} [/tex]

[tex] = {23}^{6} [/tex]

= 12^(22 - 11)

= 12¹¹

23⁴ × 23²

= 23^(4 + 2)

= 23^6
