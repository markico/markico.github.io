║SIMPLE PAST-AFFIRMATIVE NEGATIVE INTERROGATIVE║

Answer

1. (+) Dana visited some clubs in Indonesia last year

(-) Dana did not visit some clubs in Indonesia last year

(?) Did Dana visit come clubs in Indonesia last year?

2. (-) Ibrahim didn't read many book stories to get knowledge

(+) Ibrahim read many book stories to get knowledge.

(?) Did Ibrahim read many book stories to get knowledge?

3.(?) Did Selma sing two songs on the concert last night

(+) Selma sang two songs on the concert last night.

(-) Selma did not sing two songs on the concert last night.

4. (?) Did Farah write a letter to her pen pal friend ?

(+) Farah wrote a letter to her pen pal friend.

(-) Farah did not write a letter to her pen pal friend.

5. (+) Yesterday, my family went to downtown

(-) Yesterday, my family did not go to downtown

(?) Did my family go to downtown yesterday? or Did your family go to downtown yesterday?

Discussion

Struktur ke lima kalimat di atas menggunakan simple past verbal sentence { Kalimat verba bentukan lampau / simple past }. Bisa dicermati pada kata kerja aksi lampau nya { visited, wrote, read, sang & went } dan keterangan waktu { last night, last year & yesterday }

Maka daripada itu, dalam pembentukan kalimat negatif dan interrogative menggunakan kata kerja bantu / auxiliary verb "did."

Explanation

SIMPLE PAST TENSESimple past tense mendeskripsikan tindakan yang telah dimulai dan diselesaikan juga pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau . Durasi waktu kegiatan tidak ditentukan, bisa saja baru - baru ini telah terjadi dan kejadian yang memang sudah lama terjadi di masa lampau.

Berdasarkan struktur kalimatnya, dibagi 2 jenis yaitu verbal sentence & Nominal sentence .

1. Verbal sentence

merupakan struktur kalimat yang predikatnya berupa kata kerja aksi. Contoh : eat, run, drink, do, walk, read, watch, swim and etc.

Pattern of Verbal Sentence

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1 + Object/ Complement

(?) Did+Subject+Verb 1 + Object/Complement ?

2.Nominal sentence

memiliki struktur kalimat dimana predikatnya tidak terdapat kata kerja aksinya. Pada umumnya diikuti kata benda (noun), kata sifat (adjective) dan keterangan ( adverb ).

Pattern of nominal sentence :

(+) Subject + tobe ( was/were ) + noun/adjective/adverb

(-) Subject + tobe(was/were) + not + noun/adjective/adverb  

(?) tobe(was/were)+Subject + noun/adjective/adverb?

ADVERB OF TIME

( Keterangan waktu identifikasi bentuk simple past)

1. Definite point in time ( waktu penentu jelas )

last week, yesterday, two years ago, last night, this morning

Example :

Verbal

(+ ) She went to Bali  two months ago.

( - ) She did not go to  Bali  two months ago.

( ? ) Did she go to Bali two months ago?

Nominal

(+ ) We were in Paris  two years ago

( - ) We  were not in Paris two years ago

(? ) Were we in Paris two years ago  ?

2. An indefinite point in time ( dalam waktu yang tidak ditentukan )

a long time ago, the other day

Example :

Verbal  

(+ ) He lived with his grandparents  when he was young.    

(- )  He did not live with his grandparents  when he was young.

( ? ) Did he live with his grandparents when he was young?

Nominal

(+) He was an army  a long time ago      

(- ) He wasn't an army a  long time ago

( ? ) Was he an army a long time ago ?
