semisal e = harga mobil dalam kondisi excellent, n = harga mobil dalam kondisi very nice.

e = $62,125

[tex]e = 1\frac{3}{4}n \\ e = \frac{(4\times 1)+3}{4}n\\ e = \frac{7}{4}n \\ n = \frac{4}{7}e \\ n = \frac{4}{7} \times (62125) \\ n = 35500[/tex]

Jadi, harga mobil dalam kondisi very nice $35,500
