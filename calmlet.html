1. how did a hamlet called lisawa look like?


Soal ini berkaitan dengan cerita Telaga Biru (The Origin of Telaga Biru). Berikut saya sertakan juga teks narrative tentang Origin of Telaga Biru.


Pembahasan

Origin of Telaga Biru

Hemisphere North Halmahera precisely in the region of Galela hamlet Lisawa, amid the tranquility of life and the number of people who are still rare (only consists of a few houses or dadaru), resident Lisawa snapped uproar with the discovery of water suddenly came out from between the rocks proceeds freezing hot lava , The stagnant water was then formed a clear bluish telaga and is under the thick banyan tree. This incident makes people confused. They wonder from where the water source? Is this a blessing or a sign that something bad will happen. What on earth makes this phenomenon occur?

News about the formation of the lake was spreading quickly. Especially in the area were relatively hard water. Various methods are used to reveal the curiosity of the population. The traditional ceremony was held to shed light on the emergence of the small lake. Search through traditional rituals such as calling on the spirits of ancestors come to worship Jou Jou GIKI Moi or maduhutu (one God or Allah the Creator) was performed.

Customary rituals produce answers "Arising from Sininga irogi de itepi Sidago congo dalulu de i Uhi imadadi majobubu ake" (Arising from the consequences of a broken heart-broken to pieces, tears, flowing and flowing into the water source).

Dolodolo (gong) was rung as a signal that all the villagers gathered Lisawa. They hurried to come and listen to the results to be delivered by the traditional elders. The atmosphere was turned into silence. Only the sound of the breeze and sigh breath sounds population.

Elders with full authority to ask "Those of you who were not present but not at home". The population began to look at each other. Each busy counting the number of family members. Of the amount is not much it easy to know that there are two families who lost members. Because reluctant to mention the names of the children, they just greeted with a general call people Galela the Majojaru (Ms) and Magohiduuru (nyong). At first glance then, they talked about the children.

Majojaru already two days away from home and had not yet returned. Relatives and friends have been contacted, but there was no news story. It can be said that the departure Majojaru still a mystery. Magohiduuru news of the parents said that their child is six months away from home to the country people but still there was no news of when it will return.

Majojaru and Magohiduuru were lovers. When Magohiduuru leave to go abroad, both have promised to remain as lively as-as dead. Indeed, though the seasons change, months and years passed but the relationship and their love will once and for all. If it is not better to die than to live with a lie.

Six months after the departure of Magohiduuru, Majojaru remained faithful waiting. However, the storm apparently threw the love boat anchored in the middle of endless beaches it.

The news about Magohiduuru finally heard in the hamlet Lisawa. Like a bolt of lightning slammed Majojaru daytime perforated and plummet. As if he could not believe when I heard that Miss deng Magohiduuru so balaeng laeng. Promise to be as lively as a boomerang-as dead as death.

In a state that is very passionate Majojaru try to find shelter while tenderly to her. He sat under a tree shade Banyan while lamenting the love story.

The tears were unstoppable like dikes and dams were separated, the water continues to flow to uncover, waterlogged and sank the sharp rocks which were under the banyan tree. Majojaru finally sunk by her own tears.

Small lake was formed. The water is clear as tears and the color blue as pupils miss endo Lisawa. Lisawa villagers was mourning. They promised to maintain and preserve the lake they named Blue Lake.

6. X: why is he panic Y: he is oanic because his name....... By principal a. Is called b. Was called c. Are called d. Were called e. To be called


He is panic because his name is called by principal. The answer is (a). Simak penjelasan berikut.

Pembahasan>> Materi tentang Passive Voice

Bentuk dasar semua tenses yang diubah ke Passive Voice adalah BE + V3.

Contoh kalimat

       ACTIVE        ------>        PASSIVE

>> PRESENT

I water the flower -----> The flower is watered by me

I am watering the flower -----> The flower is being watered by me

I have watered the flower -----> The flower has been watered by me

I have been watering the flower -----> The flower has been being  watered by me

NOTE: Untuk subjek jamak, maka be nya adalah 'ARE'

Contoh:

I buy two books.

PASSIVE:  Two books are bought (by me).

>> PAST

A thief stole my wallet -----> My wallet was stolen by a thief

A thief was stealing my wallet -----> My wallet was being stolen by a thief

A thief had stolen my wallet -----> My wallet had been stolen by a thief

The police had been watching a fugitive -----> A fugitive had been being watched by the police.

>> FUTURE

He will drive the car -----> The car will be driven by him

He will be driving the car -----> The car will be being driven by him

He will have driven the car -----> The car will have been driven by him

He will have been driving the car -----> The car will have been being driven by him

Ingat!! Pelaku atau subjek dari kalimat pasif bisa dihilangkan.

Contoh:

Someone stole my phone --- > My phone was stolen.

The Question

X: Why is he panic?

Y: He is panic because his name ..... by principal

a. is called

b. was called

c. are called

d. were called

e. to be called

Answer

Jawaban yang paling sesuai adalah (a). Dalam dialog tersebut, pertanyaan berbentuk simple present. Dengan demikian, jawaban untuk mengisi titik-titik tersebut juga memiliki bentuk simple present.

Arti dialog Y: Dia panik karena namanya dipanggil kepala sekolah.
