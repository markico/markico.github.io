7. dialog bahasa inggris tentang reuni


Sincan : ah.. Futaba, Is that you?? It's so good to see you again! How long has
it been? Twenty years!
Futaba : Long time no see, buddy. I'm so glad I come to this reunion. I had the feeling you would be here.
Sincan : I wouldn't miss it. Wow, you look so fabulous with the red dress.
Futaba : Thanks. It's not every day that we have our twentieth reunion. 
Sincan : Yeah. Why don't we have a seat and catch up? I'm sure you've got
plenty of stories.
Futaba : I'm sure you do, too. Let's eat and drink some juice and exchange
stories.
Sincan : Ok. so, how’s everything ?
Futaba : Good, not bad. what about you?
Sincan : well, everything is fine. Now I work in the XX company.
Futaba : I'm not surprised. You always had a good head for figures. You went
into business, right?
Sincan : Yeah, you're right. so, enough about me. What about you?
Futaba : Now I teach in SMAN 3 South Jakarta as an English teacher.
Sincan : You were kind of a nerd. always reads books and finally become a
teacher.
Futaba : Hey, I was not. I was also good in speaking.
Sincan : I know. I'm just kidding. You were always worried about being called a
nerd.
Futaba : It's been great seeing you again.
Sincan : You, too Futaba. I wish you all the best.
Futaba : Thanks. I'm so happy with this reunion.
Sincan : me too.

24. sambutan singkat kepala sekolah dalam acara reuni akbar menggunakan bahasa Sunda


Sambutan singkat biantara dalam rangka acara reuni akbar:

Bismillahirrahmanirrahim

Assalamualaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh.

Anu kapihatur pangersa Bapa Wakil Kepala Sekolah sareng ka sadaya rengrengan Bapa Ibu Guru anu ku sim kuring dipihormat tur dipikacinta. Oge teu hilap ka sugri alumni SMA 01 Tumaritis, ti ngawitan angkatan ka hiji dugi ka angkatan ka duapuluh sadayana anu ku sim kuring dipikareueus.

Dina dinten ieu anu pohara agem tur agungna, urang sasarengan hadir di tempat ieu, hiji tempat anu ku urang sadayana dipikacinta. Tempat anu mangrupikeun kawah candradimuka kanggo urang sadayana, dina raraga nyambut sareng ngahontal cita-cita urang sewang-sewangan. Atuh parantos sababaraha taun urang lulus ti SMA anu dipikacinta ieu nya urang ayeuna kempel deui didieu, ngajalin asih, nyambungkeun duduluran antara urang anu tos lami teu patepung sono, ngaraketkeun deui kanyaah sareng papada babaturan sakelas, sasakola. Acara ieu mangrupikeun agenda lima taunan, tepung alumni akbar, anu mudah-mudahan tiasa janten pangbeberah manah kanggo sadaya siswa siswi sareng guru-guru, yen SMA ieu teh parantos ngahasilkeun alumni-alumni anu pohara mangpaatna kanggo bangsa sareng nagara Indonesia.

Atuh numawi kitu, sim kuring salaku Kepala Sekolah anu oge kaleresan pisan alumni angkatan kadua ti sakola ieu, ngahaturkeun wilujeng sumping, wilujeng tepang sono. Hayu urang nanjeurkeun kajayaan nu gilang gumilang kanggo ieu sakola, kanggo ayeuna sareng saterasna.

Sakitu anu kapihatur, hapunten dina samudaya kalepatan.

Wassalamualaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh.  

Pembahasan

Teks di atas adalah contoh pidato sambutan singkat dalam rangka reuni akbar di sebuah sekolah. Teks pidato atau biantara dalam bahasa Sunda isinya sama dengan pidato dalam bahasa lainnya yaitu:

Ucapan salam. Pendahuluan. Isi pidato. Penutup. Permutasi dari reuni
