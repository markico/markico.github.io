a. Volume kubus = 8 cm³

b. Volume kubus = 27 cm³

____________________.• Penjelasan Soal :

[tex] \boxed{ \tt{Volume \: Kubus} = p \times l \times t}[/tex]

[tex] \tt{a. \: panjang \: kubus = 2 \: cm} \\ \tt{lebar\: kubus = 2 \: cm} \\ \tt{tinggi \: kubus = 2 \: cm} \\ \\ > p \times l \times t \\ = 2 \: cm \: \times 2 \: cm \: \times 3 \: cm \\ \bold{= 8 \: {cm}^{3} }[/tex]

[tex] \tt{b. \: panjang \: kubus \: = 3 \: cm} \\ \tt{lebar \: kubus \: = 3 \: cm} \\ \tt{tinggi \: kubus = 3 \: cm} \\ \\ > p \: \times l \times t \\ = 3 \: cm \: \times 3 \: cm \: \times 3 \: cm \\ = \bold{27 \: {cm}^{3}}[/tex]

[tex] \tt{c. \: panjang \: kubus = 6 \: cm} \\ \tt{lebar \: kubus = 6 \: cm} \\ \tt{tinggi \: kubus = 6 \: cm} \\ \\ > p \times l \times t \\ = 6 \: cm \times 6 \: cm \times 6 \: cm \\ = \bold{216 \: {cm}^{3}}[/tex]
