18. Thenji makes a fruit salad for breakfast at a restaurant. She uses pieces of fruit in the following ratio: Banana: apple: paw - paw = 1/2 / 3 a. If she uses 20 pieces of apple, how many pieces of banana and paw-paw should she use? b. If she uses 12 pieces of banana, how many pieces of apple and paw-paw should she use?please don't spam, the others will think my question already answered :(​


Jawab:

a. She should use 10 pieces of banana and 30 pieces of paw-paw.

b. She should use 24 pieces of apple and 36 pieces of paw-paw.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Number of pieces of banana = b

Number of pieces of apple = a

Number of pieces paw-paw = p

b : a : p = 1 : 2 : 3

Question a

a = 20

b = (1/2) × 20 = 10

p = (3/2) × 20 = 30

So, she should use 10 pieces of banana and 30 pieces of paw-paw.

Question b

b = 12

a = (2/1) × 12 = 24

p = (3/1) × 12 = 36

So, she should use 24 pieces of apple and 36 pieces of paw-paw.
