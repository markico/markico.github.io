6. Tolong Yang Task 7 Sama Individual Task​


Jawaban:

Degrees of Comparison:

1. Iron is more useful than other metals.

2. My English teacher is as handsome as Andy Lau.

3. His English is getting worse and worse.

4. The older he gets, the wiser he becomes.

5. Ann does not swim so quickly as her coach claims.

6. Which is more Important, grammar or vocabulary?

7. Staying at home is more comfortable than going holiday abroad.

8. The less money you spend, the more you can save.

9. The new job is the most challenging one that I have had.

10. This mini-bus driver is much kinder than any other driver.

Individual Task

1. Her dress is prettier than mine.

2. Cheetahs are the fastest animals in the world.

3. Eating fruit and vegetables is healthier than eating fast food.

4. I think drinking milk is better than coffee.

5. China has more people than any other countries in the world.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Degrees of Comparison (derajat perbandingan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Degrees of Comparison:

Kita menambahkan akhiran "-er" dan "than" pada kata sifat satu suku kata  dan awalan "more" dan "than" pada kata sifat lebih dari dua suku kata untuk membentuk Comparative Degree (gelar perbandingan), contohnya:

Bigger than = lebih besar daripada (satu suku kata dari "Big")

More Beautiful than = lebih cantik dari pada (empat suku kata "Beautiful")

Kita menambahkan akhiran "-est" pada kata sifat satu suku kata dan awalan "The most" pada kata sifat lebih dari dua suku kata untuk membentuk Superlative Degree (derajat superlatif),

contohnya:

Biggest = terbesar (satu suku kata "Big")

The most Beautiful = tercantik (empat suku kata "Beautiful")

Semoga membantu ya.

Pembahasan

Task 1

What are these? Where do you usually find them?

Answer : These are some job vacancy advertisements which we usually find  in the newspaper.

Task 2

What would you do if you are interested in one of those vacancies? I would find out more about the company and the job description, then prepare an application letter and cover letter.What would you need to prepare if you are about to apply for the vacancies?  We need to prepare the application letter or resume and cover letter and also other supported document, such as certificates.What would you probably wear if you got a call for an interview? It is better to wear something polite and formal  with neutral color to the interview and making sure that our overall appearance neat and clean.How would you describe yourself if asked by the interviewer? I will convince the recruiter that I have the skills, knowledge and experience for the job and also will fit the working atmosphere in that company and the job description. For example "I am confident that my background and experiences would benefit this company. I am a confident person who can work well independently and in a team."How would you submit your resume or application letter based on the pictures? It is best to submit the resume to the address/email stated in the advertising. For the first and second advertisement, they asked us to send the resume either by email or to their company address. Penjelasan:

rumus simple present verbal (kata kerja)

(+) subject + V1 (s/es)

(-) subject + do/ does + not + V1

(?) do/ does + subject + V1

NOTE:

do = untuk subject they, we , i, you

does= untuk subject she, he, it

V1 + s/es hanya jika subject she, he, it

rumus simple present Nominal (kata sifat, kata benda, kata bilangan, kata ganti, kata keterangan)

(+) subject + is/am/are + nominal

(-) subject + is/am/are + not + nominal

(?) is/am/are + Subject + nominal

NOTE:

is= untuk subject she, he, it

am= untuk subject I

are= untuk subject You, they, we
