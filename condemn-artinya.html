Jawaban:

1. Finite verbs are verbs that show actions with different tenses (present, past, future), and can have a subject. Therefore, option D is correct.

2. Non-finite verbs are verbs without a subject, and they can be either infinitive verbs (with "to" in front) or gerunds (ending in "-ing"). Therefore, option A is correct.

3. The word "gossip" appears in option A and C, but only option C includes the word "disagree," which is the Pro's position in the discussion. Therefore, option C is correct.
