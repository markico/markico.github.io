25. tolong bantukan kk buat bsm trims ya ​


Gambar (a)

[tex]2x = 46 \\ x = \frac{46}{2} \\ x = 23[/tex]

[tex]3y = 39 \\ y = \frac{39}{3} \\ y = 13[/tex]

Gambar (b)

[tex]3x = x + 32 \\ 3x - x = 32 \\ 2x = 32 \\ x = \frac{32}{2} \\ x = 16[/tex]

[tex]4y = 180 - 48 \\ 4y = 132 \\ y = \frac{132}{4} \\ y = 33[/tex]
