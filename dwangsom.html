15

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Brother = b

Sister = s

Chenzom's family= b = 2s

Wangzom's family= 3b = s

'the number of sisters than brother and number of chenzom sister and wangzom brother is equal that is 3' means

Chenzom's sister = 3, wangzom's brother = 3

chenzom's family= 2b = s. s = 3. b = 2 x 3 = 6

wangzom's family. 3b = s. b = 3. s = 3 x 3 = 9

The number of girls in both family are = 6+9 = 15
