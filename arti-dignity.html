Tingkatan kebenaran yang dimaksud adalah: 1. Kebenaran universal, yaitu kebenaran yang diakui semua orang, tanpa memandang latar belakang sosial, kultural, ataupun agama. 2. Kebenaran relatif, yaitu kebenaran yang bersifat subjektif dan berubah berdasarkan konteks tertentu. 3. Kebenaran kontekstual, yaitu kebenaran yang selalu berubah mengikuti perubahan konteks dan situasi yang terjadi. 4. Kebenaran moral, yaitu kebenaran yang berasal dari nilai-nilai moral yang berlaku di masyarakat. 5. Kebenaran akhir, yaitu kebenaran yang berasal dari keyakinan spiritual tertentu.

Penjelasan:

"It is important for teenager to have self-dignity"

As teenagers, we are at a crucial stage in our lives where we are trying to figure out who we are and what we want to become. It is during this time that we need to have a strong sense of self-dignity, which is defined as the quality of being worthy of respect and self-esteem.

Having self-dignity means that we value ourselves and our opinions, and that we believe that we are worthy of being treated with respect and kindness. It also means that we are confident in our abilities and are not afraid to express ourselves and pursue our goals.

In today's world, where there are so many external influences trying to shape our identities and tell us what we should be and do, it is more important than ever for teenagers to have self-dignity. Without it, we can easily fall victim to peer pressure, bullying, and other negative influences that can harm our mental health and well-being.

On the other hand, having self-dignity can help us to build healthy relationships, make good decisions, and achieve our goals. It can also give us the confidence and resilience we need to face the challenges and opportunities that come our way.

In conclusion, having self-dignity is an essential part of being a teenager. It is not something that we are born with, but something that we need to develop and nurture. By valuing ourselves and believing in our abilities, we can build a strong foundation for our future and live happy, fulfilling lives.

Meskipun peran martabat manusia dalam kerangka aturan hukum telah menarik perhatian ilmiah selama beberapa waktu, hanya baru-baru ini pengadilan di AS telah menunjukkan minat yang sama dalam mengeksplorasi keberadaan dan tingkat hak martabat. Sayangnya, pernyataan hukum yudisial yang secara eksplisit atau implisit membangkitkan gagasan tentang hak atas martabat telah melakukannya secara ad hoc dan belum menjelaskan seperti apa kontur hak martabat tersebut. Pasal ini menyajikan empat kemungkinan tentang apa yang seharusnya dimaksudkan oleh pengadilan A.S. ketika mereka merujuk pada martabat orang tersebut. Ia mencatat bahwa masing-masing kemungkinan ini didasarkan pada pemahaman teoretis yang sangat berbeda tentang hak-hak martabat. Artikel tersebut kemudian diakhiri dengan mendesak agar pengadilan menggabungkan salah satu dari kemungkinan ini (dan menawarkan saran yang mana dari kemungkinan ini) sehingga landasan hukum yang lebih aman dapat dibangun untuk pengembangan hak martabat di AS.
