3. Zakiya painted wall with beautiful flower lastmonth. The negative form of this sentenceis ...Zakiya wasn't paint wall with beautifulflower last monthZakiya isn't paint wall with beautiful flowerlast monthZakiya weren't paint wall with beautifulflower last monthOZakiya didn't paint wall with beautiful flowerlast month​


The negative form of this sentence is Zakiya didn't paint wall with beautiful flower last month.

Pembahasan

Past Tense

➥ bentuk waktu yang menyatakan suatu perbuatan yang terjadi di masa lampau (bersifat sederhana).

Adverb of Time

➢ in 2009

➢ last month ✔️

➢ last Saturday

➢ last week

➢ last year

➢ two days ago

➢ yesterday

Rumus

1. Bentuk Verbal

☞ (+) S + V2 + O

☞ (-) S + did + not + V1 + O ✔️

☞ (?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

2. Bentuk Nominal

☞ (+) S + Be (was/were) + C

☞ (-) S + Be (was/were) + not + C

☞ (?) Be (was/were) + S + C + ?

Subject + Be

✎ I, She, He, It + was

✎ You, We, They + were

______________________________

Penyelesaian

Zakiya painted wall with beautiful flower last month.

➩ Zakiya mengecat dinding dengan bunga yang indah bulan lalu.

Ini merupakan bentuk kalimat past tense positif, tepatnya past tense positif bentuk verbal karena mengandung kata kerja, yaitu painted . Untuk mengubah kalimat ini menjadi bentuk negatif, kita harus mengikuti ketentuan yang berlaku.

☞ S + did + not + V1 + O

V1 Ω paint ✔️

V2 Ω painted

V3 Ω painted

Zakiya did not paint wall with beautiful flower last month.

Apabila did not disingkat menjadi didn't, maka :

Zakiya didn't paint wall with beautiful flower last month.

➩ Zakiya tidak mengecat dinding dengan bunga yang indah bulan lalu.

______________________________

Kesimpulan

So, the negative form of this sentence is Zakiya didn't paint wall with beautiful flower last month.

6. besar uang aufa adalah 4 kali uang dafiya, sedangkan selisih uang aufa dan dafiya adalah rp 36.000,00. jumlah uang aufa dan uang dafiya adalah


uang Aufa = A
uang Dafiya = D

A = 4D

A - D = 36.000
4D - D = 36.000
3D = 36.000
D = 12.000

A = 4D = 4 × 12.000 = 48.000

A + D = 48.000 + 12.000 = 60.000

Jadi, jumlah uang aufa dan uang dafiya adalah Rp60.000

Aufa = 4 Dafiya
A = 4D
A : D = 4 : 1

A - D = 36000

Jumlah uang Aufa dan Dafiya = __?

Jumlah uang Aufa dan Daviya
= (A + D)/(A - D) × 36000
= (4 + 1)/(4 - 1) × 36000
= 5/3 × 36000
= 5 × 12000
= 60000 → jawaban

11. •»Melita and zakiya has decided to play badminton•»Melita and zakiya have decided to play badmintonmana gays yang bener??​


Jawaban:

Melita and zakiya have decided to play badminton

Penjelasan:

• karena Subjek jamak yaitu ada 2 orang (Melita dan Zakiya)

• Melita dan Zakiya sebagai Subjek memiliki kata ganti They (mereka)

Structure kalimat:

Subjek = Melita and Zakiya (they)

Has/have = have (karena Subjek jamak)

Verb 3 = decided

Object / Complement = to play badminton

Rumus PRESENT PERFECT TENSE:

(+) S + Has/Have + VERB 3 + O/C

(-) S + Has/Have + not + VERB 3 + O/C

(?) Has/Have + S + VERB 3 + O/C?

Catatan:

I, you, we, they = have

He, she, it = has
