Jawaban:

(+) Iriana is sleeping in her bathroom

(-) Iriana isn't/is not sleeping in her bathroom

(?) Is Iriana sleeping in her bathroom?

Penjelasan:

A tag question is a short question added to a statement. The tag includes a pronoun and its matching form of the verb be, or auxiliary verb. If the tag question is negative, we shorten the phrase, or use a contraction with the auxiliary verb.

Example :

Nancy : That's a picture of your cats, isn't it?

Nancy's brother : Yes it is, that picture is from when they were 1.
