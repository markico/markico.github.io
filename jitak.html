30. nomor 6kalo ngasal dijitak tsunade di akhirat kelak​


Diketahui:

TV // PQ

RW tegak lurus PQ

PQ = 30 cm

QV = 15 cm

QR = 25 cm

RU = 6 cm

Ditanyakan:

Luas PQVT

Jawab:

RV = QR - QV

RV = 25 cm - 15 cm

RV = 10 cm

TV/PQ = RV/RQ

TV/30 cm = 10 cm/25 cm

TV = (2/5). 30 cm

TV = 12 cm

RW/RU = PQ/TV

RW/RU = 30 cm/12 cm

RW/6 cm = 5/2

RW = (5/2). 6 cm

RW = 15 cm

Luas PQVT = Luas PQR - Luas TVR

= (1/2). PQ. RW - (1/2). TV. RU

= (1/2). 30 cm. 15 cm - (1/2). 12 cm. 6 cm

= 225 cm² - 36 cm²

= 189 cm²
