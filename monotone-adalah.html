4. Ms. Patter, a minimalist, gives four tips on how to start your minimalist wardrobe. First, there is no exact limit for how many items of clothing should be owned by a minimalist. Second, the most important for decluttering your wardrobe are: choose well and make it last. Third, being minimalist doesn’t mean that you have to wear only monotone colors, be colorful and versatile. Lastly, be considerate, do not just chuck your unwanted stuff into the landfill, but try to donate, gift, or sell them first. As a minimalist it is very important to recycle and increase the value of waste since protecting the environment is also one of the goals of minimalists. Based on the text above, decide which of the following statements are true! a. These tips come from a person who adopts a minimalist lifestyle. b. it is known that there are specific rules about the number of outfits owned by a minimalist. c. a minimalist could decide how many clothes they want to keep when they tried to declutter their wardrobes. d. patter believed that a minimalist should be able to decide what items they wanted to keep and used them for a long period of time. e. a minimalist cannot use more than one color f. patter suggested that a minimalist should choose colors that are easy to mix and match g. throwing away unwanted stuff is the last option to declutter too many clothes. h. when you are prepared to be a minimalist, it is suggested to give away items that are not needed i. saving nature is also one motivation of a minimalist j. dumping the unwanted stuff is more efficient than selling than in a thrift shop


Those are the answers for TRUE/FALSE statements

a. These tips come from a person who adopts a minimalist lifestyle. (TRUE)

b. it is known that there are specific rules about the number of outfits owned by a minimalist. (FALSE)

c. a minimalist could decide how many clothes they want to keep when they tried to declutter their wardrobes. (FALSE)

d. patter believed that a minimalist should be able to decide what items they wanted to keep and used them for a long period of time. (TRUE)

e. a minimalist cannot use more than one color (FALSE)

f. patter suggested that a minimalist should choose colors that are easy to mix and match (TRUE)

g. throwing away unwanted stuff is the last option to declutter too many clothes. (TRUE)

h. when you are prepared to be a minimalist, it is suggested to give away items that are not needed (TRUE)

i. saving nature is also one motivation of a minimalist (TRUE)

j. dumping the unwanted stuff is more efficient than selling than in a thrift shop (FALSE)

Pembahasan:

a. These tips come from a person who adopts a minimalist lifestyle. (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "Ms. Patter, a minimalist"

b. it is known that there are specific rules about the number of outfits owned by a minimalist. (FALSE)

~ Statement ini FALSE atau SALAH, di teks disebutkan "First, there is no exact limit for how many items of clothing should be owned by a minimalist." No exact limit artinya tidak ada batasan pasti

c. a minimalist could decide how many clothes they want to keep when they tried to declutter their wardrobes. (FALSE)

~ Statement ini FALSE atau SALAH, di teks disebutkan "...choose well and make it last", artinya pilih dengan baik dan yang bertahan lama. Jadi hanya baju-baju tertentu saja yang bisa dipilih.

d. patter believed that a minimalist should be able to decide what items they wanted to keep and used them for a long period of time. (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "...choose well and make it last"

e. a minimalist cannot use more than one color (FALSE)

~ Statement ini FALSE atau SALAH, di teks disebutkan "Third, being minimalist doesn’t mean that you have to wear only monotone colors, be colorful...", artinya bukan berarti kamu harus memakai warna monoton saja, jadiah berwarna.

f. patter suggested that a minimalist should choose colors that are easy to mix and match (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "be colorful and versatile", arti kata versatile adalah serbaguna atau mudah untuk dimix-match

g. throwing away unwanted stuff is the last option to declutter too many clothes. (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "do not just chuck your unwanted stuff into the landfill," artinya: jangan hanya membuang barang-barang anda yang tidak diinginkan ke tempat pembuangan sampah,

h.when you are prepared to be a minimalist, it is suggested to give away items that are not needed (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "...but try to donate, gift, or sell them first."

i. saving nature is also one motivation of a minimalist (TRUE)

~ Statement ini TRUE atau BENAR, di teks disebutkan "environment is also one of the goals of minimalists, artinya lingkungan juga merupakan salah satu tujuan dari minimalis.

j. dumping the unwanted stuff is more efficient than selling than in a thrift shop (FALSE)

~ Statement ini FALSE atau SALAH, di teks disebutkan "...but try to donate, gift, or sell them first." Efisien berarti berdayaguna atau tepat, sedangkan membuangnya langsung bukanlah solusi tepat, tetapi cobalah untuk menyumbangkan, memberi hadiah, atau menjualnya terlebih dahulu.
