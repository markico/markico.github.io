18. Jika Bobot B= 60 N, Berapa berat bobot A?


Jawab

Misal:

Bobot B = w = 60 N

Bobot A = F = ?

Lk = 40 cm

Lb = 20 cm

[tex] \frac{w}{f} = \frac{lk}{lb} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{60 \: newton}{f} = \frac{40 \: cm}{20 \: cm} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{60 \: newton}{f} = 2[/tex]

[tex]f = \frac{60 \: newton}{2} [/tex]

[tex]f = 30 \: newton[/tex]

F = 30 N
