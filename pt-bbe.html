Jawaban:

1. In a year, an employees has twelve days annual leave. If she/he receives the annual leave bonus, she/he will have 17 (seventeen) days annual leave.

2. The text is about special annual leave bonus announcement.

Penjelasan:

1. Perhatikan kalimat berikut.

The eligble employees will receive an additional five (5) days annual leave, credited on January 15. The leave bonus will be accounted for separately and will remain available until used, not withstanding any other limitation of the total number of days of annual leave that may be carried forward.

Bila diartikan: Karyawan yang memenuhi syarat akan menerima tambahan cuti tahunan lima (5) hari, dikreditkan pada 15 Januari. Bonus cuti akan dihitung secara terpisah dan akan tetap tersedia sampai digunakan, tidak menahan batasan lain dari total jumlah hari cuti tahunan yang dapat dilakukan ke depan.

Jadi pada soal nomor 1, seorang karyawan mempunyai 12 hari cuti tahunan. Jika ia menerima bonus cuti tahunan, dia akan mempunyai 17 hari cuti tahunan.
