3. 1590 dibagi 14 dengan cara porogapit


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]\dfrac{1590}{14} = 10\cdot \dfrac{159}{14}\\\\\dfrac{1590}{14} = 10\cdot \dfrac{140 + 14+5}{14}\\\\\dfrac{1590}{14} = 10\cdot (10 + 1 + \tfrac{5}{14})\\\\\dfrac{1590}{14} = 110+10\cdot \dfrac{5}{14}\\\\\dfrac{1590}{14} = 110+ \dfrac{25}{7}[/tex]

[tex]\dfrac{5}{7} = \dfrac{50}{7} \cdot \dfrac{1}{10}\\\dfrac{5}{7} = \left(7+\dfrac{1}{7}\right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{10}\\\dfrac{5}{7} = \left(7+\dfrac{10}{7}\cdot \dfrac{1}{10}\right) \cdot \dfrac{1}{10}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.7 + \left(1 + \dfrac{3}{7}\right)\dfrac{1}{100}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.71 + \dfrac{30}{7}\cdot\dfrac{1}{1000}\\\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.71 + \left(4+\dfrac{2}{7}\right)\cdot\dfrac{1}{1000}\\[/tex]

[tex]\dfrac{5}{7} =0.714+ \dfrac{20}{7}\cdot\dfrac{1}{10000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.714+\left(2+\dfrac{6}{7}\right)\cdot\dfrac{1}{10000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.7142+\dfrac{60}{7}\cdot\dfrac{1}{100000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.7142+\left(8+\dfrac{4}{7}\right)\cdot\dfrac{1}{100000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.71428+\dfrac{40}{7}\cdot\dfrac{1}{1000000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.71428+\left(5+\dfrac{5}{7}\right)\cdot\dfrac{1}{1000000}\\\dfrac{5}{7} =0.714285 + \dfrac{5}{7}\cdot \dfrac{1}{1000000}[/tex]

karena 5/7 muncul lagi maka digit setelah 85 akan diulang lagi mulai dari 7, sehingga :

[tex]\dfrac{5}{7} =0.714285714285714285\hdots = 0.\overline{714285} \to \dfrac{25}{7} = 3.5\overline{714285}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\dfrac{1590}{14} = 110+ \dfrac{25}{7} = 113.5\overline{714285}= 113.\overline{571428}}[/tex]

catatan : garis diatas angka menandakan bahwa angka tersebut akan terus berulang selamanya

jari jari(r) = diameter/2 = 10/2 = 5

tinggi = 12

V = π × r × r × t

V = 3,14 × 5 × 5 × 12

V = 942 cm³

-------------------

Diketahui kerucut :

jari-jari(r) = diameter/2 = 10/2 = 5

s = 13

tinggi = √s²- r²

tinggi = √13² - 5²

tinggi = √169-25

tinggi = √144

tinggi = 12

V = 1/3 × 3,14 × 5 × 5 × 12

V = 1/3 × 942

V = 314 cm³

---------------------

diketahui setengah bola :

jari-jari (r) = diameter/2 = 10/2 = 5

V = ½ × 4/3 × π × r × r × r

V = ½ × 523,33

V = 261,66 cm³

Volume total = 942 + 314 + 261,66

U6 = -5

S30 = ... ?

Jadikan persamaan :
U7 + U10 : (a + 6b) + (a + 9b)

         U6   : a + 5b = -5         ......... persamaan 2

Eliminasi :
2a + 15b = -30 | x1 |  2a + 15b = -30
  a +   5b = -5   | x2 |  2a + 10b = -10
                                _____________ (-)
                                             5b = -20
                                               b = -4

cari nilai a
a + 5b = -5
a + 5(-4) = -5
a - 20  -5
a = -5 + 20
a = 15

S30 = n/2 (2a + (n-1)b)
        = 30/2 (2(15) + 29(-4))

        = 15 * (-86)
