13. cos 135° + sin 135° - tan 135° =


Jawab:

cos 135° = cos (90° + 45°) = -cos 45° = [tex]-\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}[/tex]

sin 135° = sin (90° + 45°) = sin 45° = [tex]\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}[/tex]

tan 135°= tan (90° + 45°) = -tan 45° = -1

Jadi, cos 135° + sin 135° - tan 135° = [tex]-\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}[/tex] + [tex]\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}[/tex] + (-1) = -1

Jadi, jawabannya adalah -1.
