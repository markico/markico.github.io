22. quizz receh (5!+5!)–(0!^2×100)=???


=(5!+5!)–(0!^2×100)

=(1×2×3×4×5×2)–(0!^2×100)

=240–(0!^2×100)

=240–(1^2×100)

=240–(1×1×100)

=240–(1×100)

=240–100

=140

jadi, jawabannya adalah 140

24. quizz receh 5!+(0!×10)+70=???


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

5! + (0! × 10) + 70

= 5! + (1! × 10) + 70

= 5! + (1 × 10) + 70

= 5! + 10 + 70

= (5×4×3×2×1) + 10 + 70

= (20×3×2×1) + 10 + 70

= (60×2×1) + 10 + 70

= (120×1) + 10 + 70

= 120 + 10 + 70

= 130 + 70

= 200

=5!+(0!×10)+70

=(1×2×3×4×5)+(0!×10)+70

=120+(0!×10)+70

=120+(1×10)+70

=120+10+70

=130+70

=200

jadi, jawabannya adalah 200

26. Quizz receh (5!+5!)–(20×10)=???


40

___________________

°• CARA •°

(5!+5!) - ( 20 × 10) =

= ( (5×4×3×2×1) + (5×4×3×2×1) ) - ( 20 × 10 )

= ( 120 + 120 ) - 200

= 240 - 200

= 40

》KESIMPULAN 《

HASIL DARI (5!+5!)-(20×10) ADALAH 40

( 5! + 5! ) - ( 20 × 10 )

= ((5×4×3×2×1) + (5×4×3×2×1)) - ( 200 )

= ((120) + (120)) - (200)

= (240) - (200)

= 40
