2. cerita dongeng yang menggunakan simple past tensen! ​


Jawaban:

the dog and the donkey

In a house of a rich farmer, there lived a dog and a donkey.

The dog had a job and it was to guard the house from thieves and thugs.

Meanwhile, the donkey had to carry loads for him every day.

One day, they heard some noise outside the farmer house.

But the dog was sleeping under a warm veranda.

He didn’t do anything but lifted up his head for a second and went back to sleep.

“It could be thieves. Why don’t you bark?” said the donkey after hearing the noises.

“Mind your own business,” replied the dog calmly.

Wanted to save his master, the donkey refused to listen and started to bray.

Little did he know, the noises came from their snoring owner.

The donkey who accidentally woke his master up was surprised and felt bad about his action.

Angry, the owner came runnning out with a stick and started to beat the donkey with no mercy.

7. Apa perbedaan dari 2 tensen berikut? Present perfect tense: - they have lived in Jakarta present perfect continou tense: - they have been living in jakarta for a long time.


Jawaban:

- they have lived in Jakarta

maknanya mereka telah tinggal di Jakarta. kalimat ini hanya menyatakan "telah". mereka bisa saja sdh tidak tinggal lagi di Jakarta.

-they have been living in jakarta for a long time.

present perfect continues tense

mereka sdh tinggal di Jkt dan sekarang jg masih u waktu yg lama. tinggal di Jakarta adalah kegiatan yg telah dimulai di waktu lampau dan sekarang masih sedang dilakukan. kalimat seperti ini biasanya u menyatakan durasi sebuah kegiatan.

Jawaban:(Simple present tense) adalah berapa kalimat yang kita pakai saat menjelaskan hal yang terjadi sekarang atau pada hari itu dalam simple present tense terdapat tiga buah contoh kalimat.yang pertama adalah positif (+) can yang dipakai saat seorang bisa ikut suatu upacara atau acara. yang kedua adalah negatif (-) can't yang dipakai saat seorang tidak bisa mengikuti acara atau upacara. dan yang ketiga adalah qestion (?) yang berupa sebuah pertanyaan.

Penjelasan:

contoh:

(+) He can go to the cinema today

(-) He can't go to the cinema today

(?) Can He go to the cinema today ?

di atas merupakan contoh-contoh present tense menggunakan kalimat positif, negatif dan question.
