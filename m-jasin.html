No..... (Verbal Sentence)1. Does the school have a library2 ......the roosters com al dava?3 .......Johan go to bed at 09 00 pm?4. ........Siska and Putn jom the English speech contest?5. .........mSandi eat want to eat a banana?​


(Nominal Sentences)

1. I am a student

2. They (are) drivers

3. She (is) a nurse

4. (Is) he okay?

5. Jasin and I (are) happy

6. Is Ardi at the gate? No, (he isn't)

(Verbal Sentence)

1. Does the school have a library

2. (Do) the roosters com al dava?

3. (Does) Johan go to bed at 09 00 pm?

4. (Do) Siska and Putn jom the English speech contest?

5. (Does) Sandi eat want to eat a banana?

PEMBAHASAN

Nominal sentences

↪Yaitu pola kalimat yang tidak menggunakan kata kerja (verbs) pada kalimatnya melainkan to be (is, am, are).

➡Penggunaan to be:

• Is -> he, she, it

• Are -> you, we, they

• Am -> I

Verbal sentences

↪Yaitu pola kalimat yang tidak menggunakan to be, melainkan kata kerja (verbs) pada kalimatnya.

➡Penggunaan Verbs:

• Does -> he, she, it

• Do -> I, you, we, they
