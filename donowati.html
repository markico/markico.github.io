13. creat procedure of how to make donnat


Ingredients:

– 200 cc of water
– 1/2 packs of instant yeast 11 gram
– 1/2 kg of flour
– 4 tablespoons granulated sugar
– 3 tablespoons butter
– 3 tablespoons of powdered milk / condensed milk
– 2 eggs
– 2 teaspoons salt

How to make:

– Mix two eggs with a spoon.
– Enter the flour, sugar, milk, salt, and butter. Mix those materials.
– Put the yeast in the water. Stir it and then put the yeast into the dough.
– Knead until smooth and set aside in a container. Cover tightly and let it for 20 minutes.
– Take 1 tablespoon of dough and round it off. Give the middle hole.
– Do that continuously and put the dough donut you have formed into a big bowl that is coated with flour.
– Fry the donuts dough and let it until cold.
– Sprinkle with powdered sugar or chocolate.
– A simple Donut is ready to serve.

Banyak cara memilih donat adalah adalah 2^37 = 137,824 cara, dimana setiap pilihan dapat berupa memilih donat tertentu atau tidak memilih donat tertentu. Artinya, Nike memiliki 137,824 pilihan yang berbeda untuk membeli kue donat di toko tersebut.

B = himpunan rasa = (keju, coklat, kacang)

[tex]banyak \: cara \: = {b}^{a} \: = \: {3}^{3} = 3 \times 3 \times 3 = 27[/tex]

jadi, banyak cara untuk mengikat donat-donat tersebut ada 27 cara
