d) jarak = 3 km + 2 km = 5 km
c) perpindahan = √3^2 + 2^2 = √9 + 4 = √13 km
b) kecepatan = perpindahan/waktu = √13/3 km/jam
a) kelajuan = jarak/waktu = 5/3 km/jam
