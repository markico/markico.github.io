10. How do you take care of the plantation?


• Apply fertilizer

• Protect the cocoa trees from insects and diseases.

REPLACING MISSING SEEDLINGS

29. Sometimes certain cocoa seedlings do not grow well. They remain small or die.

During the months following the day when you planted your seedlings, you must always look to see whether the cocoa trees are growing well.

If you see diseased or dead cocoa trees, pull them out and burn them, and also those encircling them in case of swollen shoot disease (see paragraph 42). In their place, plant other young cocoa seedlings, from among those that you have kept in the nursery bed or in baskets.

 

Weeding and soil cover

30. Many weeds grow among the cocoa tree rows.

You must not let weeds take nourishment away from the cocoa trees.

When the cocoa trees are young, you should weed 4 or 5 times every year.

When the cocoa trees are bigger, they cast a lot of shade and so few weeds will grow. It will be enough to weed once a year. When you are cultivating be very careful not to damage the trunk and roots of the cocoa trees.

31. Between the rows of cocoa trees, you should not leave the soil bare.

You should cover the soil either with cut weeds or with palm fronds, if available.

In this way the soil is protected against sun and erosion; it stays moist and cool.

When the weeds rot, they give the soil organic matter.

You can also sow a cover crop, for instance legumes. This will give the soil good protection against sun and erosion.

17. Pak Soleh Ingin naik haji, kemudian ikut tabungan haji ONH, biaya haji pada waktu itu adalah 50jt,pak soleh sudah mengumpulkan tabungan dirumah sebanyak 20jt, kemudian sebagai tabunganpembuka ia masukkan tabungan ONH sebesar 10jt, jika bulan ini pak soleh baru memulai bukatabungan dan cicilan pertama dimulai bulan depan, maka berapa minimal cicilan yang harus iatabungkan jika dijadwalkan pak soleh akan naik haji 2 tahun lagi? Selesaikan program untukmenghitung nilai cicilan tersebut! ​


Program dengan ketentuan seperti pada soal. Solusi programnya dapat diselesaikan seperti program :

#include <iostream>

using namespace std;

int main()

{

 cout << "##  Program C++ Menghitung nilai cicilan per bulan  ##" << endl;

 cout << "============================================" << endl;

 cout << endl;

 float totbiaya,tabawal,totbulan;

 double cibel;

 cout << "Input total biaya: ";

 cin >> totbiaya;

 cout << "Input tabungan awal: ";

 cin >> tabawal;

 cout << "Input lama bulan cicilan: ";

 cin >> totbulan;

 cout << endl;

cibel = (totbiaya-tabawal)/totbulan;

 cout << "Biaya cicilan perbulannya adalah = "<< cibel<< endl;

 return 0;

}

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah

Sebelum membuat suatu program sebaiknya lakukan struktrusi masalah yang dihadapi. Strukturisasi masalah dapat digambarkan dalam ERD, diagram UML atau metode lainnya. Permasalahan sederhana seperti pada soal dapat distrukturisasi dengan merangkum permasalahan seperti berikut:
Diketahui

Total biaya = 50jt

tabungan awal = 10jt

Total bulan cicilan = 24-1 bulan =23 bulan (karena bulan pertama hanya tabungan awal)

Ditanya

Cicilan perbulan = ...?

Jawab

Tentukan rumus matematika yang akan digunakan

Total biaya yang dicicil = total biaya - tabungan awal Total biaya cicil per bulan = total biaya yang dicicil : total bulan cicilan

Setelah mengetahui rumusan matematikanya, impelementasikan rumus matematika tersebut ke dalam program seperti yang dijelaskan diatas. Setelah program selesai dan dijalankan inputkan nilai-nilai seperti yang tercantum pada keterangan diketahui agar menghasilkan hasil yang diinginkan.
