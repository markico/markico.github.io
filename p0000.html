9. hitunglah PI jika dik:gnp 500000penyusutan 50000pajak tdk langsung 30000transfer payment 10000pajak langsung 20000


Ekonomi

Pendapatan Nasional

Personal Income



Diketahui:

GNP = Rp500.000

Penyusutan = Rp50.000

Pajak tdk langsung = Rp30.000

Transfer payment = Rp10.000

Pajak langsung = Rp20.000


Ditanya:

Personal Income?


Penyelesaian:

1. Cari NNP

NNP = GNP - Penyusutan

NNP = Rp500.000 - Rp50.000

NNP = Rp450.000


2. Cari NNI

NNI = NNP - Pajak tak langsung

NNI = Rp450.000 - Rp30.000

NNI = Rp420.000


4. Cari PI

PI = NNI + Transfer Payment

PI = Rp420.000 + Rp10.000

PI = Rp430.000


Keterangan:

GNP = Gross Nasional Product

NNP = Net National Product

NNI = Net National Income

PI = Personal Income


Jawab:

Jadi, besar Personal Incomenya adalah Rp430.000
