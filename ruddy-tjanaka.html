1.My mother is cooking fried rice now.
> ini present continuous (O+am/is/are + being +v3)
> passive : fried rice is being cokked by my mother now.

2.The student study English three times a week
> ini simple present (o +am/is/are + v#)
> english is studied by student three times a week.

3.Last week Chinese celebrated "Imlek"
> ini past tense (o+ was/were?v3)
> "imlek" was celebrated by chinese last week.

4.Jovial has done her mathematics homework for an hour
> ini present perfect (o+ has/have + been +v3)
> her mathematics homework has been done by jovial for an hour.

5.Ruddy will wash his car tomorrow.
> ini simple future (o+ will + be +v3)
> his car will be washed by ruddy tomorrow.
