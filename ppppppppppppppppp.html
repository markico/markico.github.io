[tex]840 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Volume balok:

[tex] = p \times l \times t[/tex]

[tex] = 15\times 6 \times 8[/tex]

[tex] = 720 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

Volume limas segitiga:

[tex] = \frac{1}{3} \times la \times t[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{1}{3} \times 15 \times 6 \times 4[/tex]

[tex] = 120 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

V bangun = V balok + V limas segitiga = 720 + 120 = 840 cm^3

30. PPPPPPPPPPPPPPPPP BANTU ​


Jawab:

17248 cm3

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Rumus volume tabung = phi x jari-jari x jari-jari x tinggi

Rumus volume kerucut = 1/3 x phi x jari-jari x jari-jari x tinggi

Diketahui =

Jari-jari = 28 cm/2 = 14 cm

Tinggi tabung = 24 cm

Tinggi kerucut = 36 cm - 24 cm = 12 cm

Ditanya = volume total?

Jawab =

Volume tabung :

= 22/7 x 14 x 14 x 24

= 22/7 x 196 x 24

= 14784 [tex]cm^{3}[/tex]

Volume kerucut :

= 1/3 x 22/7 x 14 x 14 x 12

= 1/3 x 22/7 x 196 x 12

= 22/7 x 196 x 4

= 2464 [tex]cm^{3}[/tex]

Volume keseluruhan :

= Volume tabung + Volume kerucut

[tex]= 14784 cm^{3} + 2464 cm^{3} \\= 17248 cm ^{3}[/tex]
