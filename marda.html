10. Pak Marda memiliki 3 petak sawah dengan luas 1/2 ha,36 dm² dan,12 ca luas sawah pak Marda seluruhnya adalah


Jawaban ⇒ 5.012,36 m²

[tex]1 ha = 10.000 m^2[/tex]
[tex] \frac{1}{2} ha = \frac{1}{2} * 10.000 = 5.000 m^2[/tex]

[tex]1 dm^2 = 0,01 m^2[/tex]
[tex]36 dm^2 = 36 * 0,01 = 0,36 m^2[/tex]

[tex]1 ca = 1 m^2[/tex]
[tex]12 ca = 12 * 1 = 12 m^2[/tex]

Luas sawah seluruhnya
= 1/2 ha + 36 dm² + 12 ca
= 5.000 m² + 0,36 m² + 12 m²
= 5.012,36 m²

[tex] \textit{Tanyakan jika kurang jelas} [/tex]

Expressing opinion adalah suatu bentuk ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan pendapat seseorang terhadap sesuatu. Expressing opinion terbagi menjadi expression of asking opinion (menanyakan pendapat), expression of giving opinion (memberikan pendapat), expression of responding opinion (merespon pendapat).

Expression of Asking Opinion

- What do you think about … ?

- What’s your idea?

- How do you feel about … ?

- What’s your opinion about … ?

- What’s your view on … ?

Expression of Giving Opinion

- I think …

- I feel …

- I personally believe …

- In my opinion, …

- For me, …

Expression of Responding Opinion

- I agree with you (agree)

- I think so (agree)

- That’s a good idea (agree)

- I don’t think so (disagree)

- I disagree with you (disagree)

- I don’t think that’s a good idea (disagree)
