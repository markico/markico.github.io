8. 4. On cold winter days, Bane will... a scholarship Have been Has been getting Had been geting Have been getting


On a cold winter days, Bane will has been getting a scholarship.

Honestly, the above is so grammatically wrong… as well as your question, I truly believe you should consult with your teacher telling them the question is grammatically wrong!

If it is to be correct it will be like the following:

On a cold winter day, Bane will be getting a scholarship.

You can’t just use will + has/have + been in one sentence.

Reason:

If you are to use have been, it just sounds as if Bane is a scholarship.

If you are to use has been getting, it might work but it just doesn’t sound right.

If you are to use had been getting, had is in past tense, and I do not believe this is written in the past.

If you are to use have been getting, Bane is a singular person, so we are to use has.

Last weekend, I ... Visit my grandparents 9. My father... buy the car because he lacked of money 10. A year ago, they... popular in this city. Now , everyone know them.​


Pembahasan

Kata wasn't digunakan untuk subject "i, he, she, it"

Kata weren't digunakan untuk subject "you, we, they"

Kata didn't digunakan sebelum kata verb 1 (kata kerja 1)

Jawaban

1. Maya and Tasya weren't have english class two days ago. (karena subjek Maya dan Tasya itu 2 orang)

2. Vania didn't come on time to the school yesterday because she woke up late. (karena come itu kata kerja 1)

3. The concert last night wasn't spectacular because the committe didn't prepare it well. (karena subjeknya concert, it)

4. The shopkeeper didn't open the bookshop last monday. (karena open itu kata kerja 1)

5. Claudia was geting fever yesterday. That's why she didn't come to my birthday (karena come itu kata kerja 1)

6. The box wasn't so heavy that my litte brother was able to lift it easily. (karena subjeknya barang, it)

7. He wasn't happy when the rain suddenly poured. (karena subjeknya he)

8. Last weekend, I didn't visit my grandparents. (karena visit itu kata kerja 1)

9. My father didn't buy the car because he lacked of money. (karena buy itu kata kerja 1)

10. A year ago, they weren't popular in this city. Now, everyone know them. (karena subjeknya they)
