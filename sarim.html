17. Syepi y² + 3y = y = 3 Warga², nnti klo sy komen "Sarimi" bls "Isi 2" yak ;-; ​


Pembahasan

y = 3

y² + 3y

= 3² + 3(3)

= (3×3) + 3(3)

= 9 + 3(3)

= 9 + 9

= 18
