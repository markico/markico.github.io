12. Kuis[+50]. Apa hasil dari 10³ × 10⁹ ÷ 10⁷ 2³ × 2⁹ ÷ 2²


Jawaban dan penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1.} 10³ × 10⁹ ÷ 10⁷

    = (10 × 10 × 10) × (10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10) ÷ (10 × 10 × 10

        × 10 × 10 × 10 × 10)

    = 1.000 × 1.000.000.000 ÷ 10.000.000

    = 1.000.000.000.000 ÷ 10.000.000

    = 100.000

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.} 2³ × 2⁹ ÷ 2²

    = (2 × 2 × 2) × (2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2) ÷ (2 × 2)

    = 8 × 512 ÷ 4

    = 4.096 ÷ 4

    = 1.024

B. 2³ × 2⁹ ÷ 2²
