8. What are the causes of global warming and solutions for our earth.


Jawaban:

Global warming is caused by the increase of greenhouse gases in the Earth's atmosphere. These gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane, and water vapor, trap heat from the sun, causing the Earth's temperature to rise.There are several main causes of this increase in greenhouse gases. One is the burning of fossil fuels, such as coal and oil, for energy. This releases large amounts of carbon dioxide into the air. Deforestation and land use changes, such as the conversion of forests to agricultural land, also contribute to the increase in carbon dioxide by reducing the number of trees that can absorb the gas.

To solve these issues, we can take several actions. One solution is to switch to renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, which do not produce greenhouse gases. We can also implement policies to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels and encourage the use of electric vehicles.

Planting trees and protecting forests can also help to absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. In agriculture, we can adopt practices that reduce methane emissions from livestock, such as changing their diet and improving manure management. Overall, to solve the problem of global warming, it is important for individuals, communities, and governments to work together to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and adopt sustainable practices.

Jawaban dan penjelasan:

"Why are all solutions mixtures, but not all mixture are solutions?"

Kenapa semua larutan itu campuran, tapi gak semua campuran itu larutan?

All solutions are combination, that's to say the mixture, of two or more substances (larutan = gabungan/campuran dari 2 zat atau lebih). The solute needs to be dissolved into the solvent. (Harus larut di dalam pelarut). Example salt dissolves in water (air melarutkan garam).

If the mixture is heterogeneous (heterogen) = has substance which is inmiscible and dissimilar (zatnya tidak bisa diaduk dan gak sama dgn yang lain), for example oil and water (air dan minyak gak bisa diaduk), then it's not a solution (berarti bukan larutan)

21. Sigma Quiz......!! Give your solutions! ​


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

rumus jumlah kuadrat : ∑[1 to n](p²) = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6

rumus jumlah pangkat 1 : ∑[1 to n](p) = n(n+1)/2

rumus jumlah pangkat 0 : ∑[1 to n](p⁰) = ∑[1 to n](1) = n

∑[1 to n]((3p-2)(2p+1)) = ∑[1 to n](6p²-p-2)

                                  = 6.∑(p²) - ∑p - 2.∑1

                                  = 6.n(n+1)(2n+1)/6 - n(n+1)/2 - 2n

                                  = n(n+1)(2n+1) - n(n+1)/2 - 2n

                                  = n.(n+1).(2n+1 - 1/2) - 2n

                                 = n.(n+1).(2n + 1/2) - 2n

                                 = n( (n+1)(2n + 1/2) - 2)

                                 = n.( 1/2 . (n+1)(4n+1) - 2)

                                = 1/2 . n (4n²+5n+1 - 4)

                               = 1/2 n.(4n²+5n-3)

Sebagai salah satu bahasa yang digunakan di dunia, Bahasa Indonesia dapat kita gunakan untuk menerjemahkan atau mengalihbahasakan kata, kalimat, atau teks dari bahasa asing. Dengan pengalihbahasaan ini, kita dapat memahami kalimat atau teks dalam bahasa asing dengan lebih mudah. Dengan demikian, pesan atau informasi yang terkandung dalam kalimat atau teks tersebut juga dapat kita tangkap, tanpa menyebabkan terjadinya salah persepsi atau pemahaman tentang teks tersebut.


Pada kesempatan kali ini, soal menyajikan kita dengan dua kalimat dalam bahasa asing. Kemudian, kita diminta untuk menerjemahkan kedua kalimat tersebut ke dalam Bahasa Indonesia.
