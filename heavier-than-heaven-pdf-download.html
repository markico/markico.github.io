15. Bryan, Rexie and Marco have an average mass of 48kg. Bryan is 7 kg heavier than Rexie.Rexie is 8.5 kg heavier than Marco.What is Marco's mass?​


Rexie = y

Marco = x

Bryan = 7 kg + Rexie = 7 + y

Rexie = y = 8,5 kg + Marco = 8,5 + x

[tex] \frac{bryan + rexie + marco}{3} = 48 \: kg \\ {7 +y + 8.5 + x + x} = 48 \times 3 \\ 7 + 8.5 + x + 8.5 + 2x = 144 \\ 24 + 3x = 14.4 \\ 3x = 144 - 24 \\ 3x = 120 \\ x = 40 \: kg \\ [/tex]

So, Marco's mass is 40 kg
