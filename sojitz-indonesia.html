9. apa itu adaptasi mesofit??​


Penjelasan:

Adaptasi mesofit adalah cara adaptasi tumbuhan yang hidup di lingkungan yang tak terlalu basah dan tak terlalu kering.

Contoh adaptasi mesofit:

-Akarnya bercabang dan tumbuh pesat

-Daunnya tipis dan lebar

-Stomatanya ada di bawah daun dan berfungsi untuk menghindari penguapan berlebihan

23. Pebadaan dan persanamaan anidan dan sina - spritz


Perbedaan dan persamaan dari fact statement obat “Anidan” dan “SINA-SPRITZ” dapat diidentifikasi berdasarkan data-datanya. Berikut ini akan kakak jabarkan keterangan dari obat “Anidan” yang dilanjutkan dengan “SINA-SPRITZ”


Anidan

1. Brand of the drug: Anidan

2. Name of the drug: Paracetamol tablets

3. Content/Amount: Each tablet contains paracetamol Ph Eur 500 mg

4. Use(s): For relieve from cold and flu symptoms, feverishness, aches and pains, headache, migraine, toothache, period pain, rheumatic pain, neuralgia and sore throat.

5. Direction to use and dosage: Do not take with any other paracetamol-containing products.

Dosage:

- Adults, the elderly and young people aged 12 years or over: take 2 tablets with a drink of water. Repeat every 4 hours as require. DO NOT TAKE MORE THAN 8 TABLETS IN 24 HOURS.

- Children 6 to 12 years of age: Take 1/2 to 1 tablet with a drink of water. Repeat every 4 hours as required. DO NOT TAKE MORE THAN 4 TABLETS IN 24 HOURS.

- If symptoms persist for more than 3 days, consult your doctor.

- DO NOT GIVE TO CHILDREN AGED UNDER 6 YEARS.

6. Direction to store: Keep all medicines out of the sight and reach of children.

7. Expiration date: Not available.


SINA-SPRITZ

1. Brand of drug: Sina-Spritz

2. Name of drug: Instant hand degermer

3. Description: Kills 99.99% of E.coli, Salmonella enterica and Staphylococcus sursus (MRSA) in 15 seconds. Enhanced with moisturizers.

4. Content/Amount: 4 fl. oz. (118 ml)

5. Use(s): To kill bacteria on the hand

6. Direction to use & usage: external use only

7. Direction to store: Keep out of reach of children, Keep away from fire or flame

8. Expiration date: Not available


Pembahasan

Berikut ini adalah perbedaan dan persamaan dari kedua produk tersebut:


Difference

Types of the drug. One is paracetamol and the other is hand sanitizer.Amount of product. One is counted as per-tablet and the other is ml.Use(s). One is to relieve any kind of pain, and the other is to kill bacteria.Direction to use. One is for consumption with suggested dose and the other is for external use only.

Similarity

Direction to store. Both of the products should be stored out of the sight and reach of children.Expiration date. Both of the products does not show any expiration date in the package.
