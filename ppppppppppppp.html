[tex] = \pi \times {r}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] = 3.14 \times {600}^{2} [/tex]

[tex] = 3.14 \times 600 \times 600[/tex]

[tex] = 3.14 \times 360000[/tex]

= 11,30,400 Cm

= 11304 m

Keliling

[tex] = 2 \times \pi \times r[/tex]

[tex] = 2 \times 3.14 \times 600[/tex]

= 6.28 × 600

= 3.768 Cm

= 37,68 m
