5. Combine the two sentences using relative pronoun.1. The tall man is my uncle. His car is parked under the tree.2. the teacher is Mrs. Laura. she has punished some students.3. The handbag is the latest collection from blue sapphire. Miss Loraine buys the handbag.4. Brandon got an accident last night. you admire him so much.5. The little boy ia Damian. you just played with him.​


RELATIVE CLAUSE

1. The tall man whose car is parked under the tree is my uncle.

2. The teacher who has punished some students is Mrs. Laura.

3. The handbag which Miss Loraine buys is the latest collection from blue sapphire.

4. Brandon whom you admire so much got an accident last night.

5. The little boy whom you just played with is Damian.

ELABORATION

1) WHO

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan orang (people) dan berfungsi sebagai pengganti posisi subyek. 

Exp.

~ The teacher is Mrs. Laura. she has punished some students.

Jika digabung, akan menjadi :

➡ The teacher who has punished some students is Mrs. Laura.

➡ Relative pronoun "Who" menggantikan subyek "She."

2) WHICH

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan kata benda dan hewan (thing, animal). Berfungsi pengganti posisi subyek, obyek.

Exp.

~ The handbag is the latest collection from blue sapphire. Miss Loraine buys the handbag.

Jika digabung, akn mnjadi :

➡ The handbag which Miss Loraine buys is the latest collection from blue sapphire.

➡ Which sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan posisi obyek "it."

3) WHOM

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi orang (person), penjelas obyek kata kerjanya.

Exp.

~ The little boy is Damian. You just played with him.

Jika digabung, akn menjadi :

➡ The little boy whom you just played with is Damian.

➡ Whom sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan obyek preposisi "him"

4) WHOSE

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi kepemilikan obyek. Whose menggantikan possessive adjective determiner [her, his, their and etc]

Exp.

~ The tall man is my uncle. His car is parked under the tree.

Jika digabung, akn mnjadi :

➡ The tall man whose car is parked under the tree is my uncle.

➡ Whose sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan possessive determiner "his."

The majority of these islands are uninhabited, but those that are populated feature either sleepy local settlements surrounded by pristine beaches or first rate international seaside resort hotels. Snorkeling is almost a national pastime in the Maldives and, although all of the islands' beaches feature sparkling white sand and seas of the purest sapphire blue, there are several resorts and beaches in the Maldives that deserve further inspection. one of these is the Nika Island Resort, which is located about 74 km away from the country's capital at Male and which lies on an enchanting lagoon. Apart from snorkeling, visitors can also enjoy such diverse water activities as windsurfing, sunset fishing and canoeing. The Banyan Tree Island Resort on the North Male Atoll is another example of a stunning Maldives seaside site. This resort's beaches are the ideal venues for scuba diving, catamaran sailing and night-fishing and visitors can also enjoy water-skiing and deep sea fishing. The surf surrounding the South Male Atoll's Cocoa Island Resort is enriched by the exotic species that wander in from the nearby Kandooma Channel, and fishing here can be a thrillingexperience.
