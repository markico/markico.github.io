Jawaban:

[tex] \tt \: \blue{ {199}^{20} \times {199}^{12} \div {199}^{4} } = \pink{ {199}^{28} }[/tex]

Rumus :

[tex] \tt \: \pink ○ \: \: \: {a}^{m} \times {a}^{n} = {a}^{m + n} [/tex]

[tex] \tt \: \pink○ \: \: \: {a}^{m} \div {a}^{n} = {a}^{m - n} [/tex]

--------------------------------

Penyelesaian :

[tex] \tt \: = {199}^{20} \times {199}^{12} \div {199}^{4} [/tex]

[tex] \tt \: = {199}^{(20 + 12)} \div {199}^{4} [/tex]

[tex] \tt \: = {199}^{(32)} \div {199}^{4}[/tex]

[tex] \tt \: = {199}^{(32 - 4)} [/tex]

[tex] \tt \: = \blue{ {199}^{28} }[/tex]
