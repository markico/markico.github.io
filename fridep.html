13. My mother doesn't cook fride rice(+) dan(?) ​


my mother cook fried rice
does my mother cook a fride rice?

Ini adalah kalimat yang mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Present Tense, dilihat dari penggunaan kata doesn't.

JAWABANNYA:

[+] My mother cooks fried rice

[-] My mother doesn't cook fried rice

[?] Does my mother cook fried rice? =

Yes, she does / No, she doesn't

------------------------------------------------------

Pola kalimatnya =

[+] Subject(He/She/It/kata benda tunggal) + Verb -s/-es + Object

[-] Subject(He/She/It/kata benda tunggal) + doesn't + Verb 1 + Object

[+] Does + Subject(He/She/It/kata benda tunggal) + Verb 1 + Object?

= Yes, (Subject) does. / No, (Subject) doesn't
