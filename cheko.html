14. Cheko used number cards to teach his brother Maths. His brother picked up 4 cards labeled 1, 2, 3 and 4. What is the biggest odd number that can be formed by these 4 digits?


Jawab:4321

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Step 1: In order to form the biggest odd number, we first place the smallest odd digit in the ones place, so that bigger digits can be placed in a higher digit places.

We have, xxx1.

Step 2: 4 is the biggest number among the remaining digits, 2, 3, 4. Hence we place it in the highest digit place, that is, the thousands place.

We then have, 4xx1.

Step 3: 3 is a bigger number between the remaining digits, 2 and 3. We place it in the hundreds place.

We then have, 43x1.

Step 4: Fill the tens place with the remaining digit, 2.

Hence the biggest odd number that can be formed is 4321.
