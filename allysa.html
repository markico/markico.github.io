Pamekasan student wins a math competition in Romania Alyssa Diva Mustika, a student of SMP Pamekasan, East Java, won a gold medal at the International Mathematics competition held in Romania between March 22-29, Antara news agency reported. To reporters, Diva said he was happy to win the competition which he thought was very tight. "Thank God I won. I will study harder, "he said. Indonesia sent 10 students to participate in competitions in Romania. Diva is not the first Pamekasan student to win an international science competition. Oktavian Latief, student of SMA Negeri 1 Pamekasan won the gold medal at the 2006 International Physics Olympiad. Another student, Shohibul Maromi, won the same award in 2010. "I thank Diva for giving both Indonesia and Pamekasan a good name in the international arena," said Regent of Pamekasan Kholilurrahman, as quoted by Antara.
