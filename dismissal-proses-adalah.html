Jawaban:

1) She showed the visitors the new baby.

Passive: The visitors were showed the new baby by her.

2) We must look into this matter.

Passive: This matter must be looked into by us.

3) They told he to be quick.

Passive: He was told to be quick by them.

4) She promised him a book.

Passive: He was promised a book by her.

5) They left the wounded behind.

Passive: The wounded was left behind by them.

6) The company gave Mr. Jackson a notice of dismissal.

Passive: Mr. Jackson was given a notice of dismissal by the company.

7) The waiter handed him the bill.

Passive: He was handed the bill by the waiter.

8) The child’s aunt had brought him some warm clothes.

Passive: He has been brought some warm clothes by the child's aunt,

9) The bank is lending him the money.

Passive: He is being lent the money by the bank.

10) The principal is writing her mother a letter now​.

Passive: Her mother is being written a letter now by the principal.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat-kalimat aktif di atas berpola: Subject + verb + indirect object + direct object

Untuk mengubah kalimat di atas, susunan berubah menjadi: Indirect object + auxiliary verb + passive tense + verb 3 + direct object + by subject.

Contoh pada soal nomor 9.

Subject: The bank

Verb: is lending

Indirect object: him

Direct object: the money

Conditional Sentence merupakan struktur kalimat yang memiliki maknanyang berbeda-beda berdasarkan 3 kondisi. Dibawah ini adalah contoh-contohnya:

1. If I were the puddles, I would stay away from the road.

2. If I were you, I would did the report as soon as possible.

3. If I were on Math test, I would ask my brilliant friend to teach me.

4. If the stuff got heavy to take, I would never take it.

5. If you had obeyed the rule, you wouldn't have been dismissed.  

6. If you had spent your money on the budget, you would have survived the economic crisis.    

Pembahasan

Conditional Sentence (Kalimat pengandaian) adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang mungkin terjadi atau mungkin tidak terjadi seperti yang diharapkan. Conditional sentence terdiri dari dua klausa, yaitu:  

1. Main Clause (Induk Kalimat) dan

2. IF Clause ( Anak Kalimat )

Letak main clause bisa di depan maupun di belakang IF Clause. Perbedaan letak ini tidak mempengaruhi arti. Fungsi Conditional Sentence:

1. Untuk mengandaikan sesuatu yang terjadi dimasa yang akan datang.

2. Untuk mengandalkan sesuatu yang tidak mungkin terjadi sekarang.

3. Untuk mengandaikan sesuatu yang sudah terjadi dimasa lalu.
