Write the meaning in describing sentence! 1) candid - honest; Frank (example) 2) impartial : 3) discern : 4) ostentatious : 5) contentious : 6) reproves : 7) pessimism : 8) cursory : 9) profligate : 10) mister : 11) jocular : 12) fracas : 13) caricature : 14) corroborate : 15) precarious : Tolong ya kak...


Jawaban:

1) candid - honest; Frank (example)

He also took the moment to be very candid about his divorce from his wife.

2) impartial : fair and just

I want to give you my impartial advice.

3) discern : perceive or recognize (something)

I can discern no difference between those two objects.

4) ostentatious : designed to impress

He has an ostentatious book.

5) contentious : controversial

Our government is dealing with a contentious issue.

6) reproves : censure (someone)

That man was reproved for vandalism.

7) pessimism : a lack of hope or confidence in the future.

Her arguments showed deep pessimism over her situation.

8) cursory : hasty

He did a cursory appearance at the show.

9) profligate : wasteful in the use of resources

He was a profligate man.

10) mister : a form of address to a man whose name is not known

Please, give me some money, mister!

11) jocular : Humorous

My best friend sounded in jocular mood.

12) fracas : Quarrel

The fracas was broken up by police officers.

13) caricature : imitation of a person in the form of exaggerated drawings or pictures

That caricature was banned in many countries.  

14) corroborate : confirm

His testimony corroborates the man's account of the attack.

15) precarious : uncertain

She made a precarious living as a dancer.
