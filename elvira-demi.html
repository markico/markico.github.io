PEMBAHASAN Perintah soal:

Change into simple past tense!

Ubahlah ke dalam bentuk Simple Past Tense

Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu atau Peristiwa di masa lampau.

Rumus Simple Past Tense (+) S + Verb-2 (-) S + didn’t + Verb-1(?) Did + S + Verb-1?

KESIMPULAN

1. ms almira goes to her office by car

=> ms almira went to her office by car

Keterangan:

Goes => went

2. andian & elvira don't play football in the field

=> andian & elvira didn’t play football in the field

Keterangan:

Don’t => didn’t

3. does her mother send the letter?

=> Did her mother send the letter?

Keterangan:

does => did

4. german democratic republic builds the berlin wall

=> german democratic republic built the berlin wall

Keterangan:

Builds => built

5. we stay in the factory across the rives

=> we stayed in the factory across the rives

Keterangan:

Stay => stayed
